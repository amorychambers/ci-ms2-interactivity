About

Lost in Library will select four random unplayed games from your Steam Library and add them to the board. When you select New Game, you will see a sequence of these games presented on the screen. Once the sequence is complete, you must remember the pattern and select the correct games in the same order using the buttons below. If you succeed, the next round will start with one additional turn in the sequence. If you fail, you must restart the round, or go download the game that beat you and give it a chance!


The player wins if they successfully complete round ten. The prize for your victory is that you are absolved of any guilt for ignoring your backlog of unplayed games and are free to replay an old favourite once again. If the player loses, they will be presented with some info about the game they should have picked next that may entice you to give it a shot.

Privacy Policy

Where is my Steam ID?

Your Steam ID is a unique string of numbers that is paired with your Steam profile. Lost in Library uses this to select games from your personal library to play with in the memory game. If you have a Steam account, you can find your Steam ID by clicking on your profile; your unique Steam ID is in the URL underneath the navigation bar!

What is All Games Mode?

In a normal round of Lost in Library, you will only face the games from your Steam Library with a playtime of 0. Those games you have ignored and who are granted the chance to pique your interest, demand your attention, or exact their just vengeance. If you'd like to instead randomly select from every game in your Steam Library, check the All Games Mode option!
